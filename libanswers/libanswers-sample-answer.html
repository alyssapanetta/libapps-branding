      Q. I am interested in taking an information literacy course that is full. How do I sign up?

      Answer

      Contact the instructor for the course. Keep in mind that information literacy courses are fast-paced 8-week courses, so if the course has already started, you may have already missed a substantial amount and the instructor may recommend that you join a future section of the course instead. Allowing extra students into a course is always up to the instructor's discretion.
